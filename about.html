About

This project was created for the Website Display segment of Mastermind Science Fest 2024. Every year, Mastermind School hosts an exciting science fair, and this year's event featured an innovative website display competition. Talented students showcased their web design skills, presenting creative, functional, and interactive websites. The competition added a modern twist to the fair, highlighting the importance of technology in education.

Our site offers:

Myth Explanations: Clear, evidence-based debunking of popular misconceptions.

Interactive Trivia: Test your knowledge with fun questions after each myth.

Easy Navigation: Quickly jump to any myth with our user-friendly menu.

Engaging Visuals: Clickable images that reveal detailed information about each myth.

Explore, learn, and challenge your understanding of science with us.
